Introduction

As a researcher specializing in control systems and robotics, I have dedicated over ten years to advancing our understanding and capabilities in these fields. My research is centered on developing innovative control frameworks for autonomous tracking, capturing, and stabilizing free-floating spacecraft using space robotic manipulators. By integrating advanced machine learning techniques, my work seeks to enhance the future of space exploration technology.

On this site, you will find detailed information about my background, research interests, and professional accomplishments. I aim to share my expertise and ongoing projects, providing valuable insights into the world of control systems and robotics. Whether you're a fellow researcher, a student, or simply curious about these cutting-edge technologies, I hope you'll find the content here informative and inspiring.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you.
